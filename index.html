🧳 Essential Tips for Indian Travellers

🛂 Schengen Visa

Italy, Slovenia & Croatia are all Schengen — one visa covers all 3 countries in Itinerary 3.

🚆 Book Trains Early

Book high-speed trains 4–6 weeks ahead on Trenitalia.com or Italotreno.com. Prices double last week.

🚗 IDP Required

Indian licence alone is NOT valid in Italy. Get an International Driving Permit from your local RTO before leaving India.

🚫 ZTL Zones

Never drive into city historic centers. Cameras auto-fine you €100+ per entry, billed weeks later.

💶 Cash & Cards

Cards accepted widely but keep €50–100 cash for markets, small towns and tips.

🌡️ Beat the Heat

In Florence/Bologna: sightsee before 10am and after 5pm. Midday = gelato + AC + siesta.

🎟️ Pre-book Museums

Uffizi (Florence) and Last Supper (Milan) sell out weeks ahead in July. Book the day you buy flights.

💱 Exchange Rate
