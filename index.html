🧳 Essential Tips for Indian Travellers

🛂 Schengen Visa

Italy, Slovenia & Croatia are all Schengen — one visa covers all three countries in itinerary 3.

🚆 Book Early

Book high-speed trains 4–6 weeks ahead. Prices double in the last week. Use Trenitalia.com or Italotreno.com

🚗 IDP Required

Indian driving licence alone isn't valid in Italy. Get an International Driving Permit from your local RTO before you leave India.

🚫 ZTL Zones

Never drive into city historic centers. Cameras auto-fine you €100+. Park at the outskirts and walk or take public transport.

💶 Cash & Cards

Cards accepted widely but keep €50–100 cash for small towns, markets and tips. ATMs available everywhere.

🌡️ Beat the Heat

In Florence and Bologna: sightsee before 10am and after 5pm. Midday = gelato, air conditioning, or a pool.

🎟️ Pre-book Museums

Uffizi (Florence) and The Last Supper (Milan) sell out weeks in advance in July. Book the day you buy flights.
